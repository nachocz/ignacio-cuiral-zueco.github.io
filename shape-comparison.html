How to Play

The Goal: Match the Red target shape by sculpting the block on the right.

Why This Matters

Humans have a natural intuition for shape; we can instantly judge how closely one object resembles another. Sculptors exemplify this skill, constantly comparing a target model to the material in their hands and making adjustments until the two are almost identical.
